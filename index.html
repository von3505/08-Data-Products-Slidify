Introducing the Measurement Convertor!

Speaking to an American? This app calculates your measurement from centimetres to inches and from kilograms to pounds based on your inputs.

Quick facts: 1 kilogram roughly equals to 2.2 pounds 1 centimetre roughly equals to 0.39 inch --- .class #id

User Interface : Application - Widgets

This Measurement Convertor includes 4 widgets: numericInput - A field to enter numbers for a value in centimetres and another in kilograms actionButton - The 'Convert Now' button' provides non-reactive reactivity to refresh and convert the given values Textfield - User inputs are displayed on the right column TabPanel - A separate documentation tab provides further information about this app --- .class #id
